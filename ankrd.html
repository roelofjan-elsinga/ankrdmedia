Our first creation is a foray into the world of campus interaction. Its goal: shape how tightly-knit communities share information, no matter what it is or where they are. Through Ankrd, students connect with each other in a private environment, acting as independent moderators. The service is built for its users, so changes and improvements are always in the works based on the needs of the students that use it.
